At the beginning of my High School years, I decided to take AP Computer Science in hopes of creating my website. Fast forward to College, I ended up majoring in Computer Science, with a focal point in CyberSecurity. My main focus and studies have catered to different aspects of the field like cybersecurity consulting, CTF challenges, and cyber analysis. While my passion for cybersecurity is distinctive, I am also in the work of developing CTF challenges about cryptography and forensics.

In addition to my passion for problem-solving, I've explored the realms of acting, music and video production/editing, finding immense creative fulfillment in these pursuits. Acting offers a unique canvas for me to delve into diverse roles and emotions, while video production allows me to blend technical skills with artistic expression, crafting visually captivating narratives. My time as a Division II Tennis Player at UVA-Wise has taught me invaluable lessons in teamwork and resilience, and my enthusiasm for Mixed Martial Arts (MMA) has sharpened my discipline and strategic thinking. These experiences have not only shaped my identity but also cultivated creativity, discipline, and a well-rounded perspective, enhancing my personal journey.
